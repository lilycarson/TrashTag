TrashTag

TrashTag was born out of a love for the environment and of bringing people together to contribute to a common good. By leveraging Snapchat's API for easy login and registration, TomTom's API to add location-based services, and Google's ML API to add smart recyclable good recognition capabilities, we were able able to prove a comprehensive platform that will allow people to come together and help their planet.
